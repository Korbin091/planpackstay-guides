London's public transport system is world-class, with the Underground (Tube), buses, and river services connecting all major attractions efficiently.
Sasquatch Tip: Get an Oyster Card or use contactless payment for seamless travel on all London transport - it's cheaper than buying individual tickets and caps your daily spending.
London is exceptionally safe for LGBTQ+ travelers, with Soho being the historic heart of the community and rainbow crossings marking inclusive spaces throughout the city.

9:00 AM
