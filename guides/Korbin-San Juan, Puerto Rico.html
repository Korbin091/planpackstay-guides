Section 1: Trip Overview

Destination Information

San Juan, Puerto Rico is the vibrant capital and largest city of Puerto Rico, known for its colorful colonial architecture, historic forts, beautiful beaches, and thriving nightlife scene. As a U.S. territory, it offers tropical Caribbean vibes without passport requirements for U.S. citizens.

Weather in May

Temperature: 75-85°F (24-29°C)
Conditions: Warm and humid with occasional rain showers
Rain: 4-5 rainy days expected
Humidity: 70-80%
UV Index: Very High (9-10)

Language & Currency

Official Languages: Spanish and English
Currency: U.S. Dollar (USD)
Tipping: 15-20% at restaurants
Sales Tax: 11.5% (varies by municipality)

LGBTQ+ Safety & Tips

LGBTQ+ Friendly: San Juan is very welcoming to LGBTQ+ travelers
Condado & Santurce: Most LGBTQ+-friendly neighborhoods
Pride: San Juan Pride usually held in June
Safety: Generally safe in tourist areas, use standard travel precautions
