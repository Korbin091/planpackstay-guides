Best Time to Visit

March through April is an excellent time to visit London. Spring brings milder weather, blooming flowers in the parks, and fewer crowds than summer. You'll experience the city awakening from winter with longer days and a fresh energy perfect for exploring both indoor attractions and outdoor markets like Borough Market.

Getting Around

London's transportation system is world-class. The Underground (Tube) is the fastest way to travel, with an Oyster Card or contactless payment making it seamless. Black cabs are iconic but pricey, while Uber is widely available and often more economical. For romantic walks, the Thames Path offers beautiful riverside strolls between major attractions. Boris Bikes (Santander Cycles) are perfect for short trips and exploring parks.

Sasquatch Tip:

Download the Citymapper app before you arrive - it's absolutely essential for navigating London's transport system and will save you time and confusion throughout your trip. Also, always stand on the right side of escalators in the Tube!

Safety

London is generally very safe for tourists, including LGBTQ+ travelers. Areas like Soho, South Bank, and central London are well-patrolled and welcoming. Use common sense with valuables, especially in crowded areas like markets and tourist attractions. The city is very LGBTQ+ friendly with numerous inclusive venues and events.
