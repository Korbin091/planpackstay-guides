London Overview

Best Time to Visit

Late March to early April is an excellent time to visit London! Spring is awakening, with temperatures ranging from 45-60°F (7-15°C). You'll experience longer daylight hours, blooming flowers in the parks, and fewer crowds than summer months.

Climate for Your Visit

Expect mild spring weather with occasional rain showers. Pack layers and a waterproof jacket. Average temperatures: High 58°F (14°C), Low 42°F (6°C). Daylight from 6:30 AM to 7:30 PM.

Key Facts

  • Population: 9 million (Greater London)
  • Currency: British Pound (£) - 1 GBP = ~1.27 USD
  • Language: English
  • Time Zone: GMT (UTC+0) / BST (UTC+1 during summer)
  • Voltage: 230V, Type G plugs
  • Tipping: 10-15% at restaurants if service charge not included

Must-See Attractions

  • Borough Market - Perfect for your foodie interests
  • Tower of London and Tower Bridge
  • British Museum and Tate Modern
  • Westminster Abbey and Big Ben
  • Hyde Park and Kensington Gardens
  • Camden Market and Covent Garden
  • Soho - Heart of LGBTQ+ London

Travel Tips

  • Get an Oyster Card for easy tube and bus travel
  • Stand right on escalators, walk left
  • Many museums offer free admission
  • Book restaurant reservations in advance
  • Carry an umbrella - weather can change quickly
  • Download the Citymapper app for navigation

Safety Rating: 9/10

London is very safe for LGBTQ+ travelers. The city has strong anti-discrimination laws and a vibrant, visible LGBTQ+ community. Stay aware of your surroundings in busy tourist areas and keep valuables secure on public transport.

Transportation

  • London Underground: £2.50-£5.20 per journey
  • Bus: £1.75 per journey
  • Oyster Card daily cap: £8.50 (Zones 1-2)
  • Uber: Available throughout London
  • Bolt: Available - often cheaper than Uber
  • Free City of London: Available in the financial district
  • Boris Bikes: £2 access fee + £2 per 30 minutes

Connectivity

Recommended: Airalo eSIM for seamless connectivity. Get 1GB-20GB plans starting from $4.50. Perfect for navigation, restaurant bookings, and staying connected throughout London.

Emergency Contacts

  • Emergency Services: 999 or 112
  • Police (Non-emergency): 101
  • NHS 111: Medical advice
  • Nearest Hospital: St. Thomas' Hospital, Westminster Bridge Rd
  • US Embassy: +44 20 7499 9000

Head to Borough Market - London's oldest food market and perfect for your interests! Sample vegetarian delights, artisanal products, and international cuisine. This 1000-year-old market offers incredible Indian street food, fresh produce, and specialty coffee.
