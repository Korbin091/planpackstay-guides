Best Time to Visit

April is an excellent time to visit London! Spring brings milder temperatures, blooming flowers in the parks, and longer daylight hours. You'll experience fewer crowds than summer while enjoying pleasant weather perfect for walking tours and outdoor markets. Pack layers as London weather can be unpredictable, with occasional spring showers adding to the city's romantic charm.

Getting Around

London's public transport system is world-class. The Underground (Tube) is the fastest way to travel, with an Oyster Card or contactless payment providing easy access. Buses offer scenic routes above ground, perfect for sightseeing. Black cabs are iconic but pricier, while Uber operates throughout the city. For romantic strolls, many attractions are walkable along the Thames Path. Consider Boris Bikes for short trips through parks and quiet streets.

Sasquatch Tip: Download the Citymapper app before you arrive - it's the ultimate London transport companion! It works offline and shows you the fastest routes combining walking, tube, bus, and even Boris Bikes. Pro tip: avoid the tube during rush hours (7-9 AM and 5-7 PM) when possible - perfect excuse for a leisurely coffee break!

Safety

London is one of the world's safest major cities. The city is very LGBTQ+-friendly, especially in areas like Soho, Shoreditch, and South Bank. Standard precautions apply: watch for pickpockets in tourist areas and on public transport, stay aware of your surroundings, and keep valuables secure. The city has excellent emergency services and widespread CCTV coverage. Most areas are safe to walk at night, particularly in central London.

Day by Day Itinerary

Day 1 - Royal Welcome & Historic Highlights

Start your London love affair with iconic landmarks and royal grandeur. Today's all about getting oriented while experiencing the city's most famous sights.

☀️ MORNING
9:00 AM

📍 Tower Bridge Free

Begin with London's most iconic bridge. Walk across for stunning Thames views and photograph this Gothic masterpiece. The glass floor walkways offer thrilling perspectives of the river below.

10:30 AM

📍 Tower of London Booking Required

Explore 1,000 years of history at this UNESCO World Heritage site. See the Crown Jewels, meet the famous ravens, and learn about the tower's fascinating and sometimes dark past from the entertaining Yeoman Warders.

☁️ AFTERNOON
2:00 PM

📍 Borough Market £20-40

Your must-see destination! This 1,000-year-old market is a foodie paradise. Sample artisanal cheeses, fresh produce, and incredible vegetarian options. Perfect for a romantic lunch with diverse international flavors.

4:00 PM

📍 Southwark Cathedral Free

Visit London's oldest Gothic church building, just steps from Borough Market. This peaceful sanctuary offers a moment of quiet reflection after the bustling market experience.

🌙 EVENING
6:00 PM

📍 Thames Path Walk Free

Take a romantic sunset stroll along the Thames from London Bridge to Westminster. Pass Shakespeare's Globe, Tate Modern, and enjoy stunning views of St. Paul's Cathedral across the river.
