🌦️ Weather Forecast

Late March in London brings spring weather with temperatures ranging from 8-15°C (46-59°F). Expect occasional light rain showers, so pack layers and waterproof clothing. Days are getting longer with about 12 hours of daylight.

💂 About London

London is one of the world's most LGBTQ+-friendly cities with a vibrant queer scene, especially in areas like Soho, Shoreditch, and South London. The city offers incredible diversity, world-class museums, beautiful parks, and an amazing food scene perfect for your romantic getaway.

🏳️‍🌈 LGBTQ+ Safety & Tips

  • London is very LGBTQ+-friendly with legal protections and widespread acceptance
  • Soho is the heart of London's gay scene with numerous bars, clubs, and restaurants
  • Same-sex marriage has been legal since 2014
  • Most hotels, restaurants, and attractions are very welcoming
  • Pride in London typically occurs in late June/early July

💷 Currency & Payments

  • Currency: British Pound Sterling (£ / GBP)
  • 1 USD ≈ 0.79 GBP (rates fluctuate)
  • Contactless payments widely accepted
  • Tipping: 10-15% at restaurants if service charge not included
