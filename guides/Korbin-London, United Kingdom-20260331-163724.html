Best Time to Visit

April is an excellent time to visit London as spring brings mild temperatures, blooming flowers in the parks, and longer daylight hours. You'll experience fewer crowds than summer months while enjoying pleasant weather perfect for walking tours and outdoor exploration. Pack layers as London weather can be unpredictable, with occasional showers adding to the city's romantic atmosphere.

Getting Around

London's extensive public transport system is your best friend. The Underground (Tube) connects all major attractions efficiently. Purchase an Oyster Card or use contactless payment for convenient travel. Black cabs are iconic but pricier, while Uber and Bolt offer reliable ride-sharing options. Walking is often the fastest way for short distances, especially in central London where attractions cluster together.

Sasquatch Tip: Download the Citymapper app before arrival - it's the ultimate London transport companion, showing you the fastest routes and real-time updates for all transport modes including walking times between stations.

Safety

London is generally very safe for tourists, including LGBTQ+ travelers. The city has a strong police presence and well-lit streets. Stay aware of pickpockets in crowded tourist areas and on public transport. Emergency services are highly efficient and English-speaking throughout.

Day 1 - Royal London Introduction

Begin your London adventure with iconic royal landmarks and get oriented in this magnificent city. Today focuses on the classic sights that define London's skyline and history.

☀️ MORNING
9:00 AM

📍 Tower Bridge

Free

Start with London's most iconic bridge. Walk across the Victorian Gothic towers and enjoy stunning Thames views. The glass floor walkways offer thrilling perspectives 42 meters above the river.

11:00 AM

📍 Tower of London

£33

Explore 1000 years of royal history, see the Crown Jewels, and meet the famous Beefeater guards. Allow extra time to see the ravens and learn about the tower's fascinating dark history.

☁️ AFTERNOON
2:00 PM

📍 Thames River Walk

Free

Take a romantic stroll along the South Bank from Tower Bridge toward London Bridge. Stop for photos with HMS Belfast and enjoy street performers along the way.

4:00 PM

📍 Borough Market

£15-25

Your first taste of London's incredible food scene! Sample artisanal cheeses, fresh produce, and vegetarian delights at one of the world's oldest food markets.

🌙 EVENING

Free
