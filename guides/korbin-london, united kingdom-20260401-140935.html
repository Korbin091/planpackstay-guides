Transportation

London's extensive public transport network includes the Underground (Tube), buses, and trains, making it easy to navigate the city efficiently. Black cabs are iconic and convenient for shorter journeys, while ride-share apps like Uber operate throughout the city. An Oyster Card or contactless payment provides the most economical way to travel on public transport.

Download the Citymapper app for real-time transport updates and the fastest routes. Always stand right on escalators and have your payment method ready when boarding. The Tube stops running around midnight, but Night Tube operates on weekends on select lines.
