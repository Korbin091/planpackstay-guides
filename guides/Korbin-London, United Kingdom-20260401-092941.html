Must-See Attractions

📍 Tower of London 📍 Buckingham Palace 📍 Westminster Abbey 📍 Big Ben 📍 London Eye 📍 Tate Modern 📍 British Museum 📍 Borough Market

Getting Around: London has an excellent public transport system including the Underground (Tube), buses, and river services. An Oyster Card or contactless payment makes travel seamless. Walking is often fastest for short distances, and ride-sharing apps like Uber operate throughout the city.

Sasquatch Tip: Download the Citymapper app for real-time transport updates and the most efficient routes across London. It's incredibly accurate and includes walking times between stations.

Safety

London is generally very safe for tourists. The city is LGBTQ+ friendly with vibrant communities in areas like Soho, Shoreditch, and South London. Use common sense with valuables and be aware of your surroundings, especially on public transport and in crowded tourist areas.

Experiences

Six Must-Do Activities

📍 Borough Market Food Tour

London's oldest food market offers incredible vegetarian tastings and artisanal products. Sample gourmet cheeses, fresh bread, and international vegetarian street food from passionate vendors.

📍 Thames Evening Cruise

Romantic sunset cruise past iconic landmarks including Tower Bridge, Big Ben, and the London Eye. Perfect for couples wanting Instagram-worthy photos with the city's golden-hour skyline.

📍 British Museum Private Tour

Skip the crowds with a private guided tour of world-famous artifacts including the Rosetta Stone and Egyptian mummies. Ideal for couples who want an intimate cultural experience.
