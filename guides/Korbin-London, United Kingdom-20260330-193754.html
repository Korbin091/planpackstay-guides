Location Overview

Best Time to Visit

Spring (March-May) is ideal for London, with mild temperatures and blooming gardens. Your late March/early April timing is perfect!

Climate

Spring Weather: Temperatures 8-15°C (46-59°F), occasional rain showers, longer daylight hours. Pack layers and a waterproof jacket.

Key Facts

  • Population: 9.5 million
  • Time Zone: GMT/BST
  • Voltage: 230V (Type G plugs)
  • Tipping: 10-15% at restaurants
  • Emergency: 999

Currency & Language

Currency: British Pound (£)

Language: English

Cards: Widely accepted, contactless preferred

Cash: Useful for small vendors and tips

Must-See Attractions

🎡 London Eye & South Bank
🏛️ British Museum
🌉 Tower Bridge

🎭 West End Theaters

Travel Tips

  • Stand right on escalators - walk left
  • Download Citymapper for navigation
  • Book restaurant reservations in advance
  • Many museums offer free admission
  • Pubs close early (11pm weekdays)
  • Always carry an umbrella

Day by Day Itinerary

Day 1 - March 31: Arrival & Westminster

Morning (10:00 AM)

Arrival & Hotel Check-in

Land at Heathrow, take Heathrow Express to Paddington (15 min, £25). Check into hotel and freshen up.

Breakfast: Dishoom Covent Garden - Indian breakfast, ££, 4.5★

Afternoon (2:00 PM)

Westminster Abbey

Historic royal church with 1,000 years of history. Pre-book tickets online. Expected wait: 30 min. Entry: £27

Lunch: Ravi Shankar Restaurant - South Indian vegetarian, £, 4.3★

Evening (7:00 PM)

Westminster Bridge & Big Ben

Romantic sunset walk across Westminster Bridge. Perfect photo opportunities with Big Ben and Parliament.

Dinner: Vanilla Black - Modern vegetarian fine dining, £££, 4.4★

Optional Food Spots
  • • Mildreds Soho - Vegetarian, ££, 4.2★
  • • Sketch - Contemporary European, ££££, 4.1★
  • • Rules - Traditional British, £££, 4.3★

Day 2 - April 1: Tower & East London

Morning (9:00 AM)

Tower of London

Crown Jewels and 1,000 years of history. Book early entry online. Wait time: 45 min for Crown Jewels. Entry: £34

Breakfast: Flat Iron Square - Artisan coffee & pastries, £, 4.4★

Afternoon (2:00 PM)

Tower Bridge

Walk across iconic bridge and visit glass floor exhibition. Perfect for romantic photos. Entry: £12

Lunch: Coppa Club Tower Bridge - Modern European, ££, 4.2★

Evening (6:00 PM)

Shoreditch Exploration

Trendy neighborhood with street art and LGBTQ+ friendly bars. Perfect for evening stroll and drinks.

Dinner: Dishoom Shoreditch - Bombay café style, ££, 4.6★

Optional Food Spots
  • • Duck & Waffle - Modern British, £££, 4.2★
  • • Beigel Bake - Traditional bagels, £, 4.1★
  • • Gymkhana - Indian fine dining, ££££, 4.5★

Day 3 - April 2: Borough Market & South Bank

Morning (10:00 AM)

Borough Market

London's most famous food market! Sample artisanal cheeses, fresh produce, and vegetarian delights. Free entry, budget £30 for tastings.

Breakfast: Monmouth Coffee Company - Specialty coffee, £, 4.5★

Afternoon (2:00 PM)

Shakespeare's Globe

Historic theatre recreation. Take guided tour or catch a matinee performance. Tour: £17

Lunch: Champor-Champor Thai - Vegetarian Thai, ££, 4.4★

Evening (6:00 PM)

South Bank Walk

Romantic riverside walk from London Bridge to Westminster. Street performers and beautiful city views.

Dinner: OXO Tower Restaurant - Modern European with views, £££, 4.1★

Borough Market Tip: Visit early (10-11 AM) for best selection and fewer crowds. Many vendors offer free samples - perfect for trying before buying!

Optional Food Spots
  • • Padella - Fresh pasta, ££, 4.6★
  • • Tas Restaurant - Turkish, ££, 4.3★
  • • Aqua Shard - Contemporary British, ££££, 4.2★

Day 4 - April 3: Museums & Culture

Morning (10:00 AM)

British Museum

World-class museum with Egyptian mummies, Greek sculptures. Free entry, but book timed slots online. Allow 3-4 hours.

Breakfast: The Breakfast Club Fitzrovia - All-day breakfast, ££, 4.3★

Afternoon (2:30 PM)

Covent Garden

Historic market with street performers, boutique shopping, and romantic atmosphere. Perfect for afternoon stroll.

Lunch: Inamo - Asian fusion, ££, 4.1★

Evening (7:30 PM)

West End Show

Book tickets for Lion King, Hamilton, or Phantom of the Opera. Shows typically start 7:30 PM. Tickets: £45-85

Dinner: Clos Maggiore - French fine dining, £££, 4.4★

Optional Food Spots
  • • Hoppers Soho - Sri Lankan, ££, 4.5★
  • • J. Sheekey - Seafood, £££, 4.3★
  • • Balthazar - French brasserie, £££, 4.2★

Day 5 - April 4: Royal London & Parks

Morning (10:00 AM)

Buckingham Palace & Changing of Guard

Royal residence and ceremonial changing of guard (Wed/Sat/Sun at 11 AM). Free viewing from outside.

Breakfast: Fortnum & Mason - Traditional British, £££, 4.2★

Afternoon (2:00 PM)

Hyde Park & Speaker's Corner

Romantic picnic in the park, rent pedal boats on Serpentine Lake. Historic free speech area at Speaker's Corner.

Lunch: The Ivy Chelsea Garden - Modern British, £££, 4.1★

Evening (7:00 PM)

Soho LGBTQ+ Scene

Explore vibrant LGBTQ+ friendly bars and restaurants. Start at Old Compton Street.

Dinner: Gauthier Soho - French vegetarian fine dining, £££, 4.6★

Days 6-8: Flexible Options

Remaining days include Kew Gardens, Greenwich, Camden Market, Notting Hill, Thames cruise, and additional museum visits. Full day-by-day details available in premium version.
