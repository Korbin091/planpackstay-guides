Getting Around

London's extensive Underground (Tube) system connects all major attractions efficiently, while buses offer scenic street-level views. Use contactless payment or an Oyster Card for seamless travel, and consider walking for short distances to discover hidden gems between destinations.

Sasquatch Tip: Download the Citymapper app before arrival – it's the local favorite for navigating London's transport system and will save you time planning routes between romantic dinner spots!

Safety & Security

London is exceptionally safe for LGBTQ+ travelers with welcoming neighborhoods like Soho and Vauxhall. Stay aware of pickpockets in tourist areas and keep valuables secure, but violent crime is rare and locals are generally helpful to visitors.
